Huntrix

Neon noir. K-pop demon hunters. Game prototype & world-building.

Huntrix is a short, cinematic playground where music, choreography and supernatural drama collide. It’s built as a student project but designed with heart — because stories need voice, and this one wanted a killer chorus.

Our mission

We craft small games that feel big. Huntrix exists to give players a moment — a beat, a duel, a short scene that lingers. We focus on mood, music, and character-first design: make players care, then make them dance.
Made by Khudee. Fueled by playlists, passion , Original movie "K-Pop Demon Hunters" and late-night debugging. No demons were harmed in the making of this demo (only egos).
